The History of Comedy

Along with Hazy Mills, Herzog and Co. partnered with CNN to create the Original Series The History of Comedy, exploring, across two seasons, what makes us laugh and why, and how comedy has influenced our social and political landscape throughout history. Featuring interviews with the best comedic performers and writers of our time.

Nominated, Critic’s Choice Documentary Award, Best Ongoing Series 2018

Streaming on the CNN App, Amazon Prime and AppleTV+
