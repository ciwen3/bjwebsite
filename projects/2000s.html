The 2000’s

From executive producers Tom Hanks, Gary Goetzman and Mark Herzog, CNN's Original Series "The 2000s" explores the decade that gave us Facebook, the iPhone and other cultural and political milestones.

Bringing the Decades series into the new millennium, Herzog & Co. partnered with Playtone and CNN to create the seven-part series The 2000s. The series explores technological triumphs like social media, President George W. Bush’s war on terror and response to Hurricane Katrina, Barack Obama’s presidential election, the financial crisis, hip-hop’s rise to dominance, and a creative renaissance in television.

Streaming on the CNN App, Amazon Prime and AppleTV+
