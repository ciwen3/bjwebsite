National Comedy Center (Video Installation Interviews and Ephemera)

The National Comedy Center is the United States’ congressionally designated cultural institution dedicated to presenting the story of comedy and preserving its heritage for future generations. The Center’s exclusive collections, world-class exhibits, and cutting-edge technology are featured throughout the 37,000-square-foot attraction. Visitors are educated, inspired, and entertained by more than 50 immersive experiences as they take an interactive journey through comedy history, from early vaudeville to the latest viral memes. Featuring interviews from CNN’s ‘The History of Comedy’ as well as original interviews and video installations of archival material.
